The Recarts Story

Company Profile

Established in 1985, we are a leading wholesaler & retailer in Malaysia specializing in HP Printers and Canon Imaging Solutions. Our emphasis is on distributing, selling, servicing, trade-in and renting of advanced multi-functional digital networking imaging and document management systems. If you are looking for a reliable Copier Rental, Sales Or Support Service Provider, looks no further, we are here for you.
